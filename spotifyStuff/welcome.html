Have you missed out on recent releases?

Let's say you use Spotify and have saved dozens or hundreds of albums from your favorite artists over the years. How do you know if those artists have put out new albums recently? Spotify does a great job surfacing new singles but only seems to only tell you about new albums from artists you follow.

This app will recommend albums that have come out in the last 365 days from any musician you've saved content from previously. Ready to check it out?

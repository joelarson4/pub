Critter Minute

Instructions

Your objective: find as many matching critters as you can, as fast as you can.

  • Find matching critters -- twins or triplets!
  • Ignore which direction the critter is facing.
  • Don't worry about the critter's color and eyes either ;)
  • To select or unselect a critter, click or tap it.
  • Then, click it's twin.
  • Sometimes you'll have to find triplets or two pairs of twins.
  • Once you find all the matches, you advance to the next level.
  • How fast can you get to Level 10?

Good luck!
